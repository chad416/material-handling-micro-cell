# Punch List Log Template

## 1. Document Control

Mandatory:
- Log owner, revision, project, machine ID, current status, last review date.

Guidance prompts:
- Is this log for FAT, SAT, commissioning, or production support?

## 2. Priority Definitions

Mandatory:
- Critical: prevents safe operation or acceptance.
- Major: affects function, performance, or quality.
- Minor: documentation, labeling, polish, or non-blocking improvement.

Guidance prompts:
- Which priority levels block shipment or handover?

## 3. Punch List Table

Mandatory columns:
- ID, date opened, source, description, priority, owner, target date, status, resolution, evidence, date closed.

| ID | Opened | Source | Description | Priority | Owner | Target | Status | Resolution | Evidence | Closed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| PL-001 | YYYY-MM-DD | FAT | Example issue | Major | Controls | YYYY-MM-DD | Open | TBD | TBD | TBD |

## 4. Review Cadence

Mandatory:
- Meeting frequency.
- Required attendees.
- Escalation path.
- Closure authority.

Guidance prompts:
- Who can accept a residual open item?

## 5. Closure Requirements

Mandatory:
- Objective evidence required.
- Retest required: yes/no.
- As-built update required: yes/no.

Guidance prompts:
- Did the fix change software, drawing, HMI, network, or documentation?
